About Our Library

Sunshine Community Library has been serving local families and learners for over 20 years. We believe in equal access to information and the power of reading to transform lives.

Library Services

Book Lending

We offer a wide selection of books for all ages — from fiction and non-fiction to magazines and children's stories. Borrow up to 5 books at a time.

Computer & Internet Access

Free access to computers, printers, and high-speed internet is available for all members during open hours.

Digital Library

Our digital collection includes eBooks, audiobooks, and learning platforms you can access from home.

Upcoming Events

Storytime Saturdays

Join us every Saturday at 10:00 AM for a fun and interactive children's reading session in the kids’ corner.

Senior Book Club

A monthly book discussion group for seniors. Next meeting: August 15th at 2 PM in the reading hall.
